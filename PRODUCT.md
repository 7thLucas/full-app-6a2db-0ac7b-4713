# KYYXBOT — Product

## What It Is
KYYXBOT is a private AI assistant app for a small, trusted circle of friends. It provides an AI-powered chat interface that can answer any question, assist with coding tasks, and serve as a personal AI companion — accessible only to invited users, not the general public.

## Problem It Solves
Access to a capable, private AI assistant that feels personal and exclusive — not a public product. Friends can ask anything, get help with code, and rely on a shared AI tool without it being open to strangers.

## Target Users
A small, closed group of friends who have been granted access. Private use only.

## Core Features (Day One)
- AI chat interface: ask any question, get intelligent answers
- Coding assistance: help writing, debugging, or explaining code
- Conversational history within a session
- Private access — no public sign-up

## Brand & Tone
- Name: KYYXBOT
- Tone: Friendly, sharp, and a bit cool — feels like a smart friend, not a corporate product
- Private, exclusive vibe — it's yours, not for everyone

## Strategic Principles
- Keep it simple and fast
- Private and closed — invite-only or password-protected access
- AI-first: the chat is the product
